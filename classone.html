What is a hacker

In his origins, the word hacker defined an amateur to computers. I described a fully captivated user by computer schedule and technology. In the eighties and with the arrival of personal computers 5 (PCS) and remote access networks, this term acquired a pejorative connotation and began to be used to refer anyone to connect to a network to subreptitiously invade a or several computers with the purpose of consulting, stealing or altering the programs or data stored therein. The term is also used to refer to someone who, in addition to scheduling, enjoys shredding operating systems and programs to see how they work

The hackers are the subjects who machinated what is today the Internet as we know it, the Most hackers program and contribute to the world of Linux and most of them like Study the safety of the network and break into the systems.
